About Us

Our Strength

Today, we are a company having a strength of 500+ members with our Head Office based at Mumbai, India. The company can boast of an in-house R&D facility where we constantly aim for new innovations in terms of designs, quality and function which gives us an edge over others. At the Head Office, in Mumbai we make sure every single of our products has a widespread reach.

Our manufacturing facilities are situated at Daman (170 kms from Mumbai) and at Kandla, Gujarat. Both of these are equipped with the state-of-art technology, using the best raw materials with a dedicated team who are committed to providing the finest services. Both these plants combined, facilitate a production capacity of 1000*40ft container per annum. Whilst the Daman Plant specializes in making Insulated Lunch Boxes, Food Containers, and Bottles, our plant at Kandla dominates the manufacturing of Insulated Water Jugs and Ice Boxes.
