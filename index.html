About Us

Our Story

It is our honor to introduce you to Tokyo Plast International Limited, a front runner in the Thermoware Sector since its inception in 1986. This ambitious project was initiated by the promoters to cater to the global needs of thermo products after they sensed that there was a void of superior quality products in the market. Our unparalleled product portfolio in this industry includes Homeware and Outdoor Thermo Steel Products.

Why Pinnacle?

In 2007, our home brand “Pinnacle.” was born. Today all of our products are manufactured and distributed under this brand name. Pinnacle means “peak of success”. We strive to create best value products for our customers by following the motto - “Style with Substance”.

Awards and Recognitions

Pinnacle offers a range of exceptional products that customers enjoy at home, at offices and on-the-go! In the Global market, we export all our products to over 90 countries including U.S.A, Latin America, Germany, France, Belgium, Spain and many more.

With such a renowned global presence, Pinnacle has been awarded the Best Exporter Award for 12+ years by the Indian Government. In the last 30 years, TPIL has set a benchmark in the thermoware sector as No. 1 Exporter from India.
